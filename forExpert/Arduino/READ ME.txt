read_2pot: writes at the specified frequency, on the specified serialPort, the elapsed time in microseconds and the values of the 2 potentiometers. It is useful to test the 4 possible movements, as associated to the corresponding matlab file

pot1 pot2 mov
 L    L    1 - close
 L    H    2 - open
 H    L    3 - int
 H    H    4 - ext

read_4EMG: writes at the specified frequency, on the specified serialPort, the elapsed time in microseconds and the values of the 4 EMGs
